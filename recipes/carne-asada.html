La carnita asadaaaaa

Description

This is a my favorite plate, basically it is just grilled beef

But with the twist of having delicious sauces to dress your beef plus guacamole and a lot of other colorful and tasty stuff

Ingredients

  • 1 tomahawk
  • Grill
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • 200g of Butter

Preparation

  1. First gotta fire the grill and wait until there is not fire anymore, while you wait for the fair to chill you do step 2
  2. Spread the tomahawk with a lot of salt and pepper
  3. When there is no more fire and only the blazes are keeping the temperature you put the tomahawk into the grill
  4. In order to seal the meat you have to keep the tomahawk maximum 30 seconds on each side (up-front, down, left, right, front and the back)
  5. After that you have to cook again all the sides keeping the meat 2 to 3 minutes per side
  6. Make sure that the fat of the meat does not fire up the blazes and also make sure to keep a good temperature with a fan to air out the blazes
  7. Peal and mince the Garlic very thinly
  8. Put the butter to melt in a metal bowl and then add the garlic
  9. Mix the garlic and butter
  10. Checkout how cooked the meat is using a knife and when you get your favorite term move the meat away from the blazes
  11. Shower the meat with the melted butter
  12. Slice the meat in chunks of 1-2cm
  13. Enjoy!
